Pin Usage Reference (current as of A0.2)
=========================================

ESP32 Controller
----------------
GPIO  2  - Status LED (SF::led)
GPIO  4  - Camera flash (ESP32-CAM module)
GPIO  5  - Camera D0 / WS2812 data (board-specific)
GPIO 23  - AMB Mini UART TX (to AMB PD16 serial RX)
GPIO 34  - AMB Mini UART RX (from AMB PD15 serial TX)
GPIO 14  - AMB Mini wake GPIO (drives Mini awake/high)
GPIO 15+ - refer to config.h for HX711, INA260, PIR, etc.


AMB82-Mini (Realtek)
--------------------
PD15 - UART TX to ESP32 RX (GPIO34)
PD16 - UART RX from ESP32 TX (GPIO23)
PDxx - RTSP camera sensor channels (per Realtek SDK defaults)

Other buses/power
-----------------
I2C SDA 25 / SCL 26 – load cell amplifier & sensors
HX711: DOUT 32 / SCK 33
INA260 alert: GPIO17
PIR motion: GPIO27
NeoPixel strip: GPIO5 (default) with brightness defined in config

See config.h, pins_board.h, and ARCHITECTURE.md for the canonical mappings.
